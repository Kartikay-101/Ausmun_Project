Welcome to The Indian Summit

The Indian Summit

The Indian Summit is an organization dedicated to public speaking and raising awareness on national, local, and international issues. We provide platforms and opportunities for individuals to engage in meaningful dialogue and develop their public speaking skills by organizing events of unique nature

Our organization also promotes green initiatives and advocates for a zero-plastic lifestyle, aiming to create a more sustainable and environmentally conscious community. With a mission in confluence with the GREEN INITIATIVE, the Indian Summit shall be a completely ZERO PLASTIC event, promoting green initiatives and showcasing eco-friendly practices. Our dedication to the environment underscores our belief that cultural preservation and environmental stewardship go hand in hand.
