About Us

Australia - India bilateral relations centric Model United Nation or AUSMUN, is an initiative to engage the youth of India towards a look east policy of Hon. Prime Minister Sh. Narendra Modi.

This dialogue gains special significance where India and Australia are fast emerging as partners on all fronts, be it military alliance QUAD, sports, Cinema, technology transfer, skill development and more significantly Education in the recent past. Australia and India as partners are critical to preserving the democratic values in South Asia and the Pacific given aggressive posturing by some nations in this region.

AUS MUN is a curation based on IND-AUS partnership, promoting Australia Unlimited an initiative of Govt. of Australia. The India-Australia bilateral relationship is underpinned by shared values of pluralistic, Westminster-style democracies, Commonwealth traditions, expanding economic engagement and increasing high level interaction. The longstanding people-to-people ties, ever increasing Indian students coming to Australia for higher education, growing tourism, and sporting links, especially Cricket and Hockey, have played a significant role in further strengthening bilateral relations between the two countries.

In recent years, India-Australia relationship has charted a whole new trajectory of transformational growth. Bilateral cooperation has seen exponential growth in existing frameworks of cooperation and further expanded across a wide spectrum of new areas opening up new possibilities, both at bilateral as well as global level.

The AUS MUN is an attempt to strengthen the long standing bilateral relations between these 2 commonwealth countries.

Our VISION

AUSMUN 2024 transcends the realm of a typical Model United Nations conference. It envisions a dynamic platform where young minds from India and Australia can unite, fostering a future enriched by collaboration and a bilateral relationship. We firmly believe in the limitless potential of youth to handle the course towards a brighter tomorrow, and AUSMUN serves as a powerful catalyst to ignite their engagement with critical global affairs. Imagine a world where geographical boundaries dissolve, replaced by vibrant bridges of dialogue and shared goals. This is the essence of AUSMUN's vision – to empower young leaders from India and Australia to collaborate effectively, weaving threads of understanding that strengthens the partnership between our two nations. Through AUSMUN, they will be equipped with the necessary skills to navigate the complexities of the present global landscape. AUSMUN recognizes the pivotal role that a strong India-Australia partnership plays in ensuring. Our platform provides a unique space for young minds to delve into potential avenues of collaboration across a diverse spectrum – from the exchange of knowledge in education and technology to fostering closer ties in security and cultural exchange. By nurturing these connections, AUSMUN lays the foundation for a future where India and Australia walk hand-in-hand

OUR AIM

The primary aim of AUSMUN 2024 is to provide a dynamic platform for delegates to engage in diplomatic discourse and negotiation, embodying the evolving partnership between Australia and India. This year's conference is designed to deepen the understanding and collaboration among young delegates, aligning with the strategic vision of the "Look East" policy championed by Hon. Prime Minister Sh. Narendra Modi. By delving into the multifaceted relationship between India and Australia, AUSMUN aims to shed light on their pivotal roles in various spheres, including military alliances like QUAD, sports, cinema, technology transfer, skill development, and education. AUSMUN serves as a testament to the shared democratic values, economic engagement, and increasing high-level interactions that form the foundation of the bilateral ties between India and Australia. Through promoting Australia Unlimited, an initiative of the Australian government, AUSMUN seeks to showcase the deep-rooted connections based on pluralistic, Westminster-style democracies, Commonwealth traditions, and the expanding economic cooperation. Furthermore, the conference aims to underscore the critical role of India and Australia in upholding democratic values in South Asia and the Pacific amidst regional challenges. By celebrating the enduring people-to-people ties, the influx of Indian students to Australia for higher education, growing tourism, and strong sporting links, particularly in cricket and hockey, AUSMUN 2024 aims to fortify the robust bilateral relations between these two Commonwealth nations. Ultimately, AUSMUN 2024 endeavors to provide a platform for delegates to engage in constructive dialogue, negotiate solutions, and draft resolutions that contribute to the advancement of the India-Australia relationship and address global challenges effectively within the Model United Nations framework.
